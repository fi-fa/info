Content and Community Open Platform

The open all-sport content and community platform will include an all-sports public blockchain, allowing third parties to build their decentralized applications, using FIFA as a medium of exchange and incentivizing ecosystem community building momentum.
 

Predictive Entertainment Platform

The Quanyun Prediction and Entertainment Platform is a set of ecological services built on the Quanyun platform, the public chain and FIFA. It provides the world with the underlying and basic technical services for sports forecasting and entertainment.

Sports IP NFTSwap
